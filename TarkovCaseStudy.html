ESCAPE FROM TARKOV

THE CHALLENGE

As recently graduated, I like to put my knowledge into practice, and be able to improve. The best way to develop my UI/UX skills are to create individual small projects that could provide me more experience. Working on research provides me the opportunity to test new methods and learn from more experienced designers by using their different approaches as inspiration to this project. I have been playing Escape from Tarkov on my free time for over a year now, and decided to choose some features that needed UI/UX improvement. In this project, I will work to improve some aspects of the game accessibility, interactions & friend list issues.
